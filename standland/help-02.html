Basics

Moving around more than a minute per hour counts as 1 stand.

The app uses a combination of Stand Hours and Steps for overall tracking.
If Stand Hour data is available, it takes priority. If not, the app uses Steps to determine stand status.
With Steps, walking 80 steps or more in an hour counts as one stand.
