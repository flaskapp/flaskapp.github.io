Weather

You can add weather information to your measured workouts.
To use this feature, you need to purchase PRO and set it up for use.

Your location at the start of your workouts will be sent to Apple to retrieve weather information.
Please be aware of this before using this feature.

Weather Service

Apple Weather
Provider: Apple Inc.
Data Sources
WeatherKit

Apple Weather is designed to give hyperlocal forecasts without compromising user data.
Your location information will not be sent to any other services.

Required Environment

  • Zones v 6.1 and later
  • PRO (In App Purchase)
  • watchOS 9 and later
  • Location and network available

Settings

Get Started

  1. Launch the Zones app on your Apple Watch.
  2. Tap "Settings".
  3. Tap "Weather Settings".
  4. Tap "Enable Weather".

Set The Workout Type to Record The Weather

  1. Launch the Zones app on your Apple Watch.
  2. Tap "Settings".
  3. Tap "Weather".
None
Don't use the weather
Workouts with Route
Weather is recorded only when measuring a workout that records a route map.
All Workouts
Register the weather with every workout.

View

You can check the weather of your workout on the workout details of the iPhone app.

The units of temperature and pressure depend on the region of iPhone's settings.

Workouts measured with apps other than Zones

Please note that not all weather information can be displayed.
Please check the weather data source in the app where you registered your workout.

Troubleshooting

Depending on your usage conditions, weather information may not be available.
Please note that we cannot guarantee that weather information will be registered.

Examples of reasons why it is unavailable

  1. Location information could not be obtained
  2. The network was unavailable
  3. Weather service was unavailable
