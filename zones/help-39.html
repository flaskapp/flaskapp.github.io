Can you delete my data for me?

All data used by Zones is stored locally on your iPhone and within Apple Health.

We do not collect, store, or process your personal data on our servers, and we do not have the ability to access or retrieve it.

Therefore, there is no need to contact us to request data deletion.
To delete your data, please refer to Deleting the App / Changing Devices and follow the instructions to delete the app and remove your Health data.

About GDPR (European Economic Area Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right of access and the right to erasure. However, because Zones does not collect or process personal data, we do not hold any personal data relating to you and therefore have no data to access, modify, or delete.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchase information is managed by Apple.

Deleting the app does not remove your purchase history.

For purchase details or account management, please contact Apple directly. We do not have access to or retain any purchase information.
