1-1. Health Data

We will use Apple's APIs to read and/or write certain data to the Apple Health app on your device (the “Health Data”). Such information may include, for example:

  • Workouts
  • Heart Rate

  • Active Energy

We will use your Health Data to show you the workout details and judge the heart rate zones.

We will not use your Health Data for marketing or advertising purposes. Your Health Data will be accessed locally through the Apple Health and will not be stored or cached by the app. Your Health Data will not be sent to our server. This app cannot read from or write to the Apple Health without your consent.

1-2. Location Data

This app will start to collect location data from sources such as GPS, Wi-Fi and cell towers once you install the App and consent to the App’s tracking your location (the “Location Data”).

We will use your Location Data to show you the route map of your workout.

We will not use your Location Data for marketing or advertising purposes. Your Location Data is stored locally and not be sent to our server. This app cannot read your location without your consent. You can adjust your mobile device settings by following the instructions here.
