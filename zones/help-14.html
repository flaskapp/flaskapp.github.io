Check the Permissions of Apple Health

Zones app saves data in Apple Health.
Permission is required for use.

How to Check

iPhone

iOS 15 or later

  1. Open "Health" app in iPhone
  2. Tap "Sharing" in tab
  3. Tap "Apps"
  4. Tap "Zones"
  5. Turn on all write / read switches

iOS 13, 14

  1. Open "Settings" app in iPhone
  2. Tap "Health"
  3. Tap "Data Access & Devices"
  4. Tap "Zones"
  5. Turn on all write / read switches

iOS 12

  1. Open Apple "Health" app in iPhone
  2. Tap "Sources" of tab
  3. Tap "Zones"
  4. Turn on all write / read switches

Apple Watch

  1. Open "Settings" app in Apple Watch
  2. Tap "Health"
  3. Tap "App"
  4. Tap "Zones"
  5. Turn on all write / read switches

Setting Items


The setting items may change depending on your environment.
