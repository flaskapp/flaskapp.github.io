Reminder does not arrive

Reminders are notified using the iPhone notification.
If you don't receive reminders, check your settings.

Cube Notification Settings

  1. Tap the Settings icon on Cube app
  2. Tap "Reminders"
  3. Turn on the reminder switch you want to notify

By tapping the row, you can change the notification date and time, message and icon.

iPhone Notification Settings

  1. Tap "Go to Notification Settings" at the bottom of the reminder screen
  2. Tap "Notifications"
  3. Turn on "Allow Notifications"
