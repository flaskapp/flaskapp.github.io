No chart data available

Chart data is from Apple Health.
If you don't see any data in the chart, it may be due to missing data or lack of read permissions.

Check if data exists in Health

  1. Open "Health" app
  2. Tap "Browse"
  3. Tap "Body Measurements"
    For your resting heart rate, tap "Heart"
  4. Tap target health item
  5. Tap "Show All Data" and comfirm your data
  6. If you want to register data, tap "Add Data"
    (Wrist Temperature cannot be added)

Check health permissions for Cube

Check whether reading of the target health care item is permitted.
