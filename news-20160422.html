Announcement of PopWeight Transfer

One of our apps, PopWeight, was transferred to Metaps Inc. on April 22nd, 2016. PopWeight users will be able to continue using the app.

We really appreciate that PopWeight has been supported by a large number of users since its release in 2012. This app holds a special place in our hearts. We hope that this transfer will lead to even further growth and development of the app.
