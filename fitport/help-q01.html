FitPort does not launch

iPhone:

FitPort is integrated with Apple's' Health app. So if the Health app can not be launch, FitPort stops at the launch screen.
Please try again later, or restart your device.

Apple Watch:

Please reinstall FitPort on your Apple Watch.

  1. Open the Watch app on your iPhone, then tap the My Watch at the bottom tab.
  2. Tap FitPort on the Installed on Apple Watch section.
  3. Turn off Show App on Apple Watch
  4. Wait a little, and once FitPort is removed from your Apple Watch, turn the Show App on Apple Watch on again.
