Can I use FitPort on iPad?

Currently, FitPort is not available on iPad.

With iPadOS 17, Apple's Health app has become available on iPad, and we are currently considering making this app compatible with iPad devices.

(Enabling the app for use on iPad in its current state would make it purchasable on devices running iPadOS 16 or earlier, where the Health data is unavailable, resulting in the app's functionality being unavailable. Therefore, careful consideration is required.)
