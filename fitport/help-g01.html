FitPort needs access to the Health app

FitPort needs access to Apple's Health app (HealthKit).

You can grant or deny permissions separately for each type of data.

Write:
You can add data to the Health app
You can read FitPort's data on the Health app
Read:
You can read data on the Health app (including data from other apps)

We recommend enabling both writing and reading permissions for the items you wish to display. This app will never send your health data or personal information anywhere. Data entered within the app will be securely stored in the Health app.

How to change permissions

If you deny access, the next time you wish to change it, you will need to do so from the Health app. The following steps are for iOS 10; the process may vary slightly if you use a different OS version.

  1. Open Apple's Health app
  2. Tap Sharing at the bottom tab
  3. Tap Apps and Services
  4. Tap FitPort
  5. Tap Turn On All (or turn switches on items you want to use. Both Read & Write)
