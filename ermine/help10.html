Widget Appears Completely White or Black

After iOS updates or app updates, widgets may sometimes appear wholly white or black.

Waiting might resolve the issue and return the widget to normal. However, if you need a quick solution or the situation doesn't change even after waiting, please try the following steps:

  1. Restart your device
    • How to Restart an iPhone
    • How to Restart an iPad
  2. If the issue persists even after restarting, please try removing the widget and reinstalling it.
