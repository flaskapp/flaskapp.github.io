Widgets do not work after update (Date stays the same, contents blank)

After updating your iOS or the app, you may encounter issues with your widget. These issues include the widget's date not updating or the widget appearing completely blank (white/black).

In many cases, waiting for a while can resolve the problem. However, if you need a quicker solution or the issue persists, please try the following steps:

  1. Remove the affected widget from your home screen, then re-add it.
  2. If the problem continues, try restarting your device.
