Widgets are Not Updating

Widgets may not be updated immediately, right after placing the widget on your home screen.

Things like this may happen:

  • Event dots do not appear.
  • Holidays are not colored in the widget, even though you registered a holiday calendar.

In many cases, waiting for a while can resolve the problem. However, if you need a quicker solution or the issue persists, please try the following steps:

  1. Open the Ermine app and add a new event, or change the start day of the week, or change widget color, etc. to wake up the widgets update cycle.
  2. If this doesn't resolve the issue, please check out these steps.
